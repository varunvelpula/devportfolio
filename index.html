Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM) using GraphSLAM

Positive Drive Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

• Developed a novel design of a Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) incorporating positive drive • CVT is an automatic transmission based on belt drive driven between two pulleys at different diameters. The diameters can be adjusted dynamically to alter the speed ratio • Positive drive is achieved using a retractable gear tooth when engaged with the steel belt • The aim of the design was to improve fuel economy, increase torque transmission and improve driving experience for truck drivers
